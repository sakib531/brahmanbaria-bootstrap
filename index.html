Welcome to Brahmanbaria Community UK

At Brahmanbaria Community UK, we are more than just an organization – we are a family, united by our shared heritage and values. Representing the vibrant Brahmanbaria district, our community thrives on the principles of unity, compassion, and service.

Our Story

Established with a vision to foster a strong sense of belonging among residents of the Brahmanbaria district residing in the UK, Brahmanbaria Community UK is built on the foundation of inclusivity and mutual support. Our journey began with a successful inaugural gathering where passionate individuals came together to form a committee through a democratic process, embodying the spirit of cooperation and collective empowerment.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

At Brahmanbaria Community UK, our mission is simple yet profound – to create a nurturing environment where every member feels valued, heard, and supported. We are committed to promoting cultural heritage, fostering social cohesion, and contributing positively to both our local community in the UK and our beloved homeland, Bangladesh.

What We Offer

Brahmanbaria Community UK provides a platform for individuals to voice their opinions, participate in democratic processes, and engage in meaningful dialogue to collectively shape our future. From social gatherings to welfare activities, we offer a diverse range of events and initiatives aimed at fostering camaraderie and personal growth within our community.

Message from Advisors

President

Barrister Mohammed Wazed Ali

It is my immense pleaseure and honour to serve my beloved Brahmanbaria Community UK. I feel hugely privileged and honored to serve the Community as current President.

Our community is built on the great pillars of unity, compassion, cooperation and reciprocal respects . It is my pleasure to lead such a splendid Community comprising of many multi- talented, vibrant and dedicated individuals.

In Brahmanbaria Community UK we share and we care about our every member of the Community.

Our vision is to create a supportive , cohesive and inclusive environment where every member feels honoured , valued and heard.

As a Community together we celebrate our enriched cultural heritage, foster personal and collective growth and contribute positively to both our community based in the UK as well as that in our homeland in Bangladesh.

We have achieved much together - ranging from organising many successful gatherings to significant number of welfare and charitable activities and that has been possible through collective efforts and contributions from our Community members. But yet there is still so much to do.

As president of the Community call upon all members to stay united and encourage each of our members to actively get involved in the future community activities, share your valuable ideas through their active participation. We believe that your sincere contributions and efforts(no matter how smallor big) would make a significant difference to shape our beloved BCUK well and to support the People in Need.

I cordially you all for your continued support, cooperation and dedication. I look forward to working with all of you to further our mission and strengthen our brotherly relationship.

Thankfully,

Barrister Mohammed Wazed Ali
President, Brahmanbaria Community UK

Join Us

With over 200 proud members and counting, Brahmanbaria Community UK continues to grow, fueled by the passion and dedication of its members. Whether you're a long-standing resident or new to the community, we warmly welcome you to join us on this journey of friendship, collaboration, and shared prosperity.

BCUK is more than a non-profitable organisation; it is a movement towards a just and equitable world. Together, we can bridge the gaps of inequality, inspire hope, and transform lives across districts. Join us in our mission to make a difference, one community at a time.
